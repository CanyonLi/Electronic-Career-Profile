Research

Research Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming education, offering scalable and adaptive learning solutions that cater to individual student needs. This research paper explores how AI-powered learning companions, specifically the Menti project by Tech Insight Studio, can revolutionize education by enhancing accessibility, affordability, and personalization. In this paper, I examine the integration of machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), and data analytics into AI-driven education systems.

By analyzing Menti's design, its ability to tailor learning experiences, and its potential to reduce the reliance on traditional tutoring, this study highlights how AI can bridge learning gaps. Additionally, the paper addresses the technical, ethical, and policy challenges surrounding AI in education, including data privacy and algorithmic biases. Ultimately, this research provides insights into how AI can support student-centered learning and create a more inclusive and cost-effective educational ecosystem for the future.

PEM Fuel Cell Researcher
@2024 SARSEF & @2024 AIAA

Role Overview

  • Investigated how temperature and reactant gas purity (electrolyzed H₂/O₂ vs. ambient air O₂) impact the energy conversion efficiency of PEM fuel cells.
  • Designed and conducted 60+ independent trials, measuring voltage, current, temperature, and gas consumption data under varied operating conditions.
  • Analyzed results using linear regression, significance tests, and two-sample t-tests to confirm efficiency gains at higher temperatures and with high-purity reactants.
  • Employed strict lab safety protocols and iterative improvements to enhance data precision, reducing experimental error in real-time measurements.
  • Formulated recommendations for next-generation PEM fuel cell design and optimization in clean energy applications.

Key Skills & Accomplishments

  • Experimental Design & Data Collection: Developed robust procedures to measure and compare power output, thermal release, and reactant usage.
  • Statistical Modeling: Applied regression analysis, hypothesis testing, and ANOVA to interpret efficiency trends and validate hypotheses.
  • Technical Proficiency: Set up complex circuits with ammeters, voltmeters, variable resistors, and custom electronics for real-time monitoring.
  • Process Optimization: Identified sources of experimental error (internal resistance, instrumentation limits) and proposed solutions to improve accuracy.
  • Research Communication: Documented findings in detailed reports, complete with references to relevant literature and graphical data insights.
